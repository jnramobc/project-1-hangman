Welcome, Let's play Spaceman!

Message

Welcome, brave adventurer! Your mission is to save the spaceman by guessing the secret word. Follow these simple steps to play:

  1. Choose a Letter: Click on one of the letter buttons to make a guess.
  2. Check the Word: If the letter is in the word, it will appear in the correct position(s).
  3. Incorrect Guesses: Each wrong guess will bring the spaceman closer to being lost.
  4. Youe have until the entire spaceman body appears (6 incorrect guesses) to guess the entire word. If you run out of turns, the spaceman is lost
  5. Win or Lose: Successfully guess the word before your turns run out to save the spaceman and win the game. If not, brace yourself for an interstellar defeat.

Good luck, and may the cosmos be in your favor!
